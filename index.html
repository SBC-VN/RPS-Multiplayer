Instructions:

  • This is an online, multi-player, game of Rock Paper Scissors.
  • Please enter your screen name in the space provided to the left of these instructions, then click on either the "play" or "spectate" button.
  • If your screen name is already being used you will be prompted to change it and retry.
  • If your screen name is not being used, you will go into the lobby
  • The lobby is where you will see what's going on in the current game (if any) and participate in the chat.
  • If you have chosen to play you will be in the queue waiting for your turn to come up
  • When your turn comes, you will play the winner of the previous game.


Game Play

  • Each game will consist of 5 rounds of standard 'rock paper scissors' game play.
  • You will have 20 seconds to make a choice between the rock, paper or scissors.
  • To choose: click on the rock, paper, or scissors and then on the "throw" button.
  • Standard RPS rules apply - a tied round must be replayed and will not advance.
